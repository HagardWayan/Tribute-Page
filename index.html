Timeline of President Rodrigo Duterte:

  • 1945 - Born in Maasin, Leyte
  • 1968 - Graduated at Lyceum of the Philippines University with the degree of Bachelor of arts in Political Science
  • 1972 - Obtain a Law degree at San Beda College of Law
  • 1973 - Began working as a prosecutor for Davao City
  • 1986 - Won as a Mayor of Davao City. He served seven terms of being a Mayor of the City

The Presidency:

  • 2017 - Declaring Martial Law for the second time

    May 2017 when Muslim militants attacked Marawi City in order to prove their group to become a member of the ISIS group. Due to this incedent, President Duterte declared Martial Law in all provinces of Mindanao. After 5 months of battle Marawi was declared free from the hands of the Maute group.

    Due to environmental factors, the president ordered a temporary closure of the Boracay Island for some rehabilatation since the island is facing a massive environmental problems.

  • 2019 - The continuation

    President still continued his War on Drugs, and even named a lot of corrupt official of the country. With this corrupt officials were lessened and a lot of changes and improvements were seen in the country.

  • 2020 - The Pandemic

    The global pandemic hit the Philippines due to Covid-19. This is the first time where Phillipines experienced pandemic but President Duterte faced the challenge and did his best in order to keep the country safe as possible. Ordered lockdowns on every provinces and airports. Whats best is that, there were no reports that the country men faced hunger since the government did its best to provide foods to the people.

  • 2021 - The Pandemic 2.0

    Pandemic is still present up to this year, but unlike on 2020 pandemic this time didn't give panic to people since they are aware on what to do when they are infected with Covid-19 virus. Vaccination rate this time rised unlike on 2020 that people were afraid of covid-19 vaccines due to trust issues because of the Dengvaxia issue.
